About WHAT'S UP BREW

Read the history behind the business of Marc: inspired by a deep love for coffee and a desire to create a warm, community-centric space, Marc founded "What's Up Brew." His vision was to share not just exceptional coffee but also to provide a haven where people could connect, share stories, and find joy in the simple pleasure of a well-brewed cup. Marc aimed to foster a sense of belonging and create a welcoming atmosphere that transcends the typical coffee shop experience.
